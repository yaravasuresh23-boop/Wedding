How It All Began

Our Love Story

August 2019

First Meeting

A chance encounter at a friend's dinner party in Florence. James spilled wine on Olivia's dress — and spent the rest of the evening making her laugh about it.

December 2020

First Date

A snowy evening walk along the Arno river turned into hours of conversation over espresso. They closed down the café and kept talking on a bench until dawn.

June 2022

Moving In Together

A tiny apartment with a leaky faucet became their first home. They learned to cook together, argue about furniture, and fall deeper in love every single day.

September 2024

The Proposal

Under a canopy of stars in the Tuscan countryside, James got down on one knee. Olivia said yes before he even finished the question. Tears, laughter, and champagne followed.
