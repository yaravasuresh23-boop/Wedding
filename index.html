How It All Began

Our Love Story

August 2019

First Meeting

A chance encounter at a friend's dinner party in Florence. James spilled wine on Olivia's dress — and spent the rest of the evening making her laugh about it.

December 2020

First Date

A snowy evening walk along the Arno river turned into hours of conversation over espresso. They closed down the café and kept talking on a bench until dawn.

June 2022

Moving In Together

A tiny apartment with a leaky faucet became their first home. They learned to cook together, argue about furniture, and fall deeper in love every single day.

September 2024

The Proposal

Under a canopy of stars in the Tuscan countryside, James got down on one knee. Olivia said yes before he even finished the question. Tears, laughter, and champagne followed.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Is there parking available at the venue?
Yes, complimentary valet parking will be available for all guests. Self-parking is also available in the estate's main lot, a short 2-minute walk to the chapel.
Can I bring a plus-one?
Your invitation will indicate whether a plus-one is included. If you're unsure, please reach out to us directly and we'll do our best to accommodate.
Are children welcome?
We love your little ones! Children are welcome at the ceremony. A supervised kids' area with activities and a special menu will be available during the reception.
What if I have dietary restrictions?
Please let us know about any dietary requirements or allergies when you submit your RSVP. Our chef is happy to accommodate vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary needs.
What happens if it rains?
Don't worry — we have a beautiful backup plan! The Rosewood Grand Hall will serve as our indoor alternative, with the same romantic atmosphere and décor.
When is the RSVP deadline?
Please submit your RSVP by May 15, 2025. This helps us finalize catering, seating, and accommodation arrangements. Early responses are greatly appreciated!
